Cloud Technology Spotlight

Cloud technology enables businesses to store, manage, and process data on remote servers. It provides flexibility, scalability, and cost efficiency. With cloud platforms, users can access their resources from anywhere, collaborate in real-time, and deploy powerful computing solutions without physical infrastructure.


Infrastructure: Cloud computing depends on remote network servers hosted on the Internet to store, manage, and process data. On-Demand Access: Users can access cloud services and resources on demand, scaling up or down without having to invest in physical hardware. Types of Services: Cloud computing offers various benefits such as cost saving, scalability, reliability, and accessibility. It reduces capital expenditures, and improves efficiency. .
